RAY Club is a volunteer organization that visits an orphanage, providing friends as well as fellowship. We want the children to have time to develop relationships, rather than just receiving supplies. The ten board members gather every week to share ideas about what food and activities they need to prepare before visiting the orphange. They also work diligently to see if there are any difficulties or awkwardness among the volunteers. You can participate as a volunteer, and volunteers have the opportunity to receive Presidential and Leadership awards. From 8th grade to 12th grade, 50 volunteers visit the orphanage every two weeks to have fun and learn the importance of the family through RAY Club.

In 2017, 750 Christmas presents were presented to children of the orphanage. I would like to exten my gratitude to the students and parents who have bene prepared with love.
